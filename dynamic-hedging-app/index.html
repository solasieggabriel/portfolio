⚠️ Disclaimer & Tool Purpose

This tool is an analytical utility intended solely to help minimize potential losses and manage risk. It is not intended to promote any specific activities. Additionally, please note that the math within these strategies is best suited for sequential events. While it can be applied to simultaneous events, overlapping timelines can cause odds to fluctuate rapidly, making it more difficult to execute your risk management plan securely.

📖 Glossary of Terms

Full Slip
The original multi-leg sequence you are trying to protect.
Initial Stake
The original amount of money placed on the full slip.
Total Slip Odds
The combined multiplier if all legs in the full slip win.
Leg
An individual event or component within the full slip sequence.
Hedge
A secondary action taken against a specific leg to secure a profit or minimize loss.
Hedge Odds
The current multiplier available for betting against your leg.
Sunk Cost
The total amount of money already spent on past hedges.
Target Hedge Safety Net
The exact profit you wish to guarantee if a specific hedge wins.
